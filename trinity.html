About Trinity University

Trinity University is located in San Antonio, Texas. Founded in 1869, Trinity University has celebrated one hundred and fifty years of quality higher education. Located just three miles from downtown San Antonio, Trinty Univerity's campus lies just minutes away from many unique places and establishments, such as the San Antonio Riverwalk, the San Antonio Internation Airport, and the San Antonio Zoo. New students come from all over the world to become part of an educational institution that values the alluring learning environments nestled between the charming red brick walls.


Photo courtesy of Trinity University

What Do I like About Trinity?

The features about Trinity that I appreciate most are the numerous studying environments one can find readily on campus and summer research opportunities. From bookable rooms in Coates Library to spacious computer labs in the newly-renovated Center for Sciences & Innovation, I am sure I can find a place to be productive. I can also look forward to gaining invaluable research on-campus, where I may lay the foundations of knowledge that will help contribute to my future success in a graduate career.

I chose Trinity University because it offered a comfortable learning experience, wherein small class sizes and respected professors allow each student to build connections and understand academic material more clearly.
